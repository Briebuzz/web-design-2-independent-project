    Happy Tails and Tales

    Kieta [images/_.jpg] Kieta was born in Alaska along with her brothers, sisters, and parents. They were escorted to Ontario on a private plane flown by a very nice volunteer. Once in Ontario she was taken care of by an an amazing foster family who took very good care of her until the Buzzel family came along. They took Kieta in and gave her a loving, forever home. She now spends her days with her new sisters and best friends, running through fields and swimming at her cottage.
    Rein [images/_.jpg] Rein is a pure bred Siberian Husky born in Sudbury. She was adopted by couple who was looking to start a family. However after 3 years they were not able to continue looking after Rein and wished to surender her. Once she was surrendered the Buzzel family heard about her and wished to give her a good home. They took Rein in and gave her a loving, forever home where spends her days with her new sisters and best friends, running through fields and swimming at her new cottage.
